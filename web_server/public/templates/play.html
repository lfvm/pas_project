Tempo Artist

Instructions

There are two game modes, the purpose in both, is to hit the notes as near as posible from the hit area for keep the accuracy in 100%. And reach the maximum score.

Perfect hits give 300 points, good hits 100, poor hits 50 and missed hits 0 points.

The details of a selected level will appear in top left of screen.

You can select the speed of the notes by clicking in options button.

  • Game mode 1: Here you only have lineal notes of two types, blue notes can be hit by clicking A or L, and purple notes whith S or K.
  • Game mode 3: Here only have blue notes, this will fall in four verical rails, press A, S, K or L for each rail to hit them.

At the end of the level, the stats of the try will appear in a new screen. (Score, accuracy, max combo and perfect, good, poor, missed notes).

To see the all time stats you can click in My Stats button at the nav-bar. Here you will find graphs about your tries in all levels.
